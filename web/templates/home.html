Early Detection of Dementia using IoT and ML

Dementia is a neurodegenerative disorder that causes the loss of cognitive functioning i.e. thinking, remembering, and reasoning that affects millions of individuals worldwide, imposing a huge burden on healthcare systems and families. Early detection can significantly enhance the quality of life for those affected. The proposed system introduces a novel approach to early dementia detection by using Machine Learning and the Internet of Things. Random Forest Classification algorithm is employed to process and interpret the patient's medical history. Using the machine learning model the detection of dementia can be predicted and based on the results, further treatment is suggested by the model. If the patient does not have any dementia related medical record, then using the IoT device, we can detect and measure motion and orientation changes, and we can check and keep track of future dementia chances.
